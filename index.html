Bookshelf

Your writing companion for iPhone

What is Bookshelf?

Bookshelf is a writing and reading companion app built for authors, writers, and anyone who loves organising their words. Draft, structure, and preview your work — all from your iPhone.

Features

  • Books, Chapters, Topics & Pages — organise your writing with a flexible hierarchy that works for novels, research, journals, and more
  • Markdown Editor — write with live Markdown formatting and preview your work as polished, styled text
  • Outline View — drag and drop to rearrange chapters, topics, and pages with a visual outline
  • Export — back up your books or share them as formatted files
  • Cover Images — add custom cover art to personalise your library
  • Characters, Locations & Items — track the people, places, and things in your stories
  • Logbook — set word goals and track your writing progress over time
  • Research & Sources — keep reference material alongside your writing
  • Dark Mode — beautiful in light and dark

Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. Bookshelf collects no analytics, has no third-party SDKs, and never transmits your content. Your writing is yours alone. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
