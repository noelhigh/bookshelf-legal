Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your writing is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. If you use iCloud Backup, your app data may be included in your device backup.

Can I back up my work?

Yes. Bookshelf includes a built-in export feature that lets you save your books as files to the Files app, iCloud Drive, or any location you choose. We strongly recommend backing up regularly.

What if I accidentally delete something?

Deleted items are moved to the Bin inside the app, where you can restore them. Items in the Bin remain available until you permanently empty it.

Does Bookshelf work offline?

Yes. Bookshelf is fully offline — no internet connection is required to use any feature.

Can I use Markdown?

Yes. The editor supports Markdown formatting with live syntax highlighting. You can also preview your pages as styled, formatted text.

How do I organise my writing?

Bookshelf uses a flexible hierarchy: Books contain Chapters, which contain Topics, which contain Pages. You can drag and drop to rearrange in the Outline view, and move items between parents at any time.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Bookshelf collects no personal information, has no analytics, and includes no third-party tracking. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
